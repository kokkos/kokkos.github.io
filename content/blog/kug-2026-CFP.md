# Join the Kokkos User Group at HPSFcon 2026!

We are pleased to announce that the next Kokkos User Group meeting will be held in **Chicago, IL** on 
**March 19th and 20th**, as part of the 2nd annual **High Performance Software Foundation conference (HPSFcon)**.

## What to Expect:

- **Technical Talks:** Learn about the latest advancements in Kokkos libraries, including new 
  features, performance optimizations, and best practices.
- **User Presentations:** Share your experiences using Kokkos and learn from others in the community.
- **Poster Session:** Showcase your research and projects using the Kokkos Ecosystem.
- **Networking Opportunities:** Connect with other Kokkos users, developers, and experts in the 
  high-performance computing community.

## Who Should Attend:

- Researchers and developers using Kokkos for scientific computing, data science, and other 
  high-performance applications.
- Anyone interested in learning more about parallel programming and modern C++.
- HPC professionals looking to improve the performance and portability of their applications.

## Why Attend HPSFcon:

HPSFcon brings together a community of developers and users of open-source software for high-performance 
computing. This is a unique opportunity to learn about the latest trends and technologies in HPC and connect 
with leaders in the field.

## Call for Submissions:

We are seeking high-quality presentations and lightning talks for the **Kokkos User Group meeting (KUG)**.

### 20-minute presentations:

Share your experiences with using Kokkos in your research or applications. Topics of interest include:
- Novel applications of Kokkos libraries
- Performance optimizations and best practices
- Experiences with specific Kokkos features
- Integration of Kokkos Core, Kernels, FFT, etc., with other libraries or frameworks

### 10-minute lightning talks:

Brief presentations on a specific topic related to Kokkos, such as:
- A unique use case for Kokkos
- A challenging problem encountered while using Kokkos
- A suggestion for a new Kokkos feature or improvement

We also encourage users to bring up pain points and challenges they face while using Kokkos. This valuable feedback 
will help guide future development and improve the user experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Q: What if I only want to attend the KUG meeting?  
A: You still need to register for HPSFcon. There is no separate registration process for the KUG meeting.

### Q: Is there a virtual attendance option for the KUG meeting?  
A: Unfortunately, no. The 2026 Kokkos User Group meeting will be held **in-person** at HPSFcon in Chicago. 
This provides a valuable opportunity for face-to-face interaction with the Kokkos developer team and other
users in the community.

### Q: Will the talks and presentations from the KUG meeting be recorded and posted online later?  
A: Yes, recordings will be made available after the event. You can view last year's talks on the 
[2025 KUG YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?app=desktop&list=PLRKq_yxxHw29lHZwttFgG_zIpzAtgSl_e).
