# Why Attend?

This session is designed specifically for community members who cannot attend
the upcoming HPSF Summit in Germany or the Kokkos User Group meeting in
Chicago. It is your primary opportunity to:

* **Preview the Roadmap:** Gain insights into the leadership's goals for the
  Kokkos core and ecosystem.

* **Direct Feedback:** Share your specific needs and pain points regarding
  future development.

* **Ask Anything:** Engage in a live Q&A with the leads from major national
  labs and industry.
