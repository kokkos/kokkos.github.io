# Developers meeting 26

After the release of the Kokkos Ecosystem 5.0.0 the developers will meet this year to discuss what the new priorities will be for the evolution of the project and its libraries.

## Events Details

Dates: March 9th to 13th
Location: Sandia NM, Albuquerque, New Mexico

## Who attends?

This meeting is dedicated to the Kokkos developers team and will be available by invitation only.
